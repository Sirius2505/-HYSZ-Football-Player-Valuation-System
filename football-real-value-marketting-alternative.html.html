Valuation Methodology

Our proprietary algorithm calculates a player's real market value by analyzing their on-field performance statistics with a multi-factor model. This model goes beyond traditional metrics to include advanced data and adjusts for key variables like age, league, and disciplinary record to provide a more objective valuation. The coefficients listed below represent the value attributed to each statistic.

Key Metrics

  • Goals (Gls) A key indicator of a player's direct impact on scoring. €1.5M
  • Assists (Ast) Measures a player's ability to create scoring opportunities for teammates. €1.2M
  • Expected Goals (xG) The probability that a shot will result in a goal, based on the shot's characteristics. €800k
  • Expected Assists (xAG) The probability that a pass will become a goal assist. €600k
  • Progressive Carries (PrgC) Carries that move the ball significantly closer to the opponent's goal. €250k
  • Progressive Passes (PrgP) Passes that move the ball significantly closer to the opponent's goal. €150k

Penalties & Bonuses

  • Yellow Card (CrdY) Penalty for receiving a yellow card due to disciplinary issues. -€100k
  • Red Card (CrdR) Significant penalty for receiving a red card, indicating severe disciplinary action. -€500k
  • Age Penalty (after 25) A penalty applied for each year over the age of 25 to reflect decreasing resale value. -€500k / year
  • U21 Potential Bonus A bonus for young players under 21 with high potential for future growth. +€5M
  • Top 5 League Bonus An additional bonus for players in a top 5 European league (Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1) due to higher exposure and competition. +€10M

The model is calibrated to identify players whose calculated "real value" significantly differs from their perceived or existing market value, highlighting potential transfer opportunities.
